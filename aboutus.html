About Us

Who are we?

Rainydays is a Norwegian rain jacket brand that was established in 2024.

The idea came to us when we were out on a hike in the mountains of Trollheimen. Trollheimen is located a few miles (Norwegian miles) away from the third biggest city in Norway called Trondheim.

Trondheim lays in the county Trøndelag, and Trøndelag is known for it`s sudden change in weather, where you basicly get all of the four seasons in one and same day. And how do we dress for a weather like that? Well, you wear clothes from Rainydays ofc!

Now that we finally have launched after a ton of testing on hikes in all the four season, we are confident enough to say that we got jackets who can handle it all!

Our jacket design

Designed to withstand the Nordic climate, Rainydays jackets allow you to maintain comfort and style in both rain, wind and whatever climate you meet on your adventures. Ourjackets are designed to fullfill every need you might have going on hiking, canooing, skiing and more.

Double layerd with a airpocket inbetween to keep the air warm, and you warm. Still with breathable, but also waterproof membran. These jackets will give you the hiking experience you always have longed for, without the discomfort of being too cold, too hot or too clammy. You will now be ready to push the comfort zone in another matter of speach!
