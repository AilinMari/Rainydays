    The lightweight wind -and water proof jacket from Norway

    Monsen is an ever-contemporary unisex rain jacket delivering high functionality in a minimal silhouette. Features include double welded slanting flap pockets, adjustable cuffs and a fishtail hem. The look is finished with snap fastenings, under-arm eyelets, and a back yoke with concealed vents.

    This waterproof jacket is cut from Rainydays signature fabric and features ultrasonically welded seams. The finish is lightweight with a soft feel and sporty look.
